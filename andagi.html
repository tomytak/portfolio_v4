Grandma's Andagi

A family recipe brought to life

This visual essay shows the process of cooking a traditional Okinawan dessert— sata andagi.

Skills
  • Film Director
  • Film Editing
  • Typography
  • Layout
the challenge

Set a peaceful, yet engaging ambiance

Create a short film depicting the cooking process while capturing the spirit of the event.

the outcome

Let the music set the pace

Setting a scene that focuses mainly on her hands brings attention to the grace that is effortlessly employed when cooking. The music, in turn, reflects the pace at which she carries herself throughout the process.

Synopsis

Depicting the process of cooking the traditional Okinawan dessert—sata andagi—allowed me to preserve a moment that I've always looked forward to when I visit my grandma's house.
