Create a title sequence that captivates audiences through a sense of mystery while setting the tone for the rest of the horror movie.

the outcome

Envoke a sense of unease

Synopsis

Light is a title sequence for a fictitious horror-movie that I composed. This film is about a girl who unknowingly finds a supernatural candle after the lights go out in her town. She discovers that the smoke from the resulting flame allows entities–both good and bad–from another dimension to enter her world.
