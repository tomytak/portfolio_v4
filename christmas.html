Telescope Christmas Card

Astronomy through entertainment

The Canada-France-Hawai‘i Telescope requires a visual identity with supporting graphics for their collection of scientific videos for their annual Waimea Solar System Walk.

Skills
  • Motion Graphics
  • Animation
  • Vector llustration
  • Layout
  • Typography
the challenge

Emphasize the grandeur of our solar system

Design collateral that will draw in attendees of all ages while capturing and maintaining audience attention through motion graphics that support and enhance the video content.

the outcome

Adding value through graphics

Use a consistent illustrative style for the motion graphics and promotional collateral to inspire and capture the attention of the audience with a wide range of age groups.

Artwork

Illlustrations

To capture the attention of a heterogeneous audience, I created simplified designs with animation that depicted specific astronomical phenomena that help people understand the video content.
