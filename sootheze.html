Sootheze

Sootheze is a company that manufactures plush aroma and temperature therapy products, which aid in alleviating physical pain and mental distress across a wide spectrum of demographics.

Brand Strategy

core attributes

  • Nurturing
  • Welcoming
  • Natural
  • Theraputic
  • Teacherly

slogan

Here to hold, hold to heal

Upon researching Sootheze's brand and reviewing their use of language, I developed their core attributes, audience, and brand personality upon which I based my designs and content.

Audience

Sootheze products fill a broad demographic need. From calming and comforting preschoolers, who love to fall asleep with the warmth and comfort provided by the product; to Seniors in assisted living facilities who cuddle our bears giving them a sense of security and relief.

Brand Personality

  • Welcoming

    We welcome those who seek comforting and holistic solutions for themselves or their loved ones.

  • Teacherly

    Our language is engaging and teacherly to encourage and empower people to care for themselves and others.

  • Empathetic

    We embrace the differences between our customers and approach them with a language that is nurturing and mindful of their experiences.
