AstroDay

Science education through exploration

The Maunakea Observatories needs a new identity with promotional collateral for their annual AstroDay, which became a virtual event due to the onset of COVID-19.

Skills
  • Vector llustration
  • Typography
  • Layout
  • Logo Design
the challenge

Attract the public to a newly virtual event

Create promotional print and social media collateral for this family-friendly event that sparks excitement about astronomy.

the outcome

A different event with a recognizable look

Design an identity similar to the Waimea Solar System Walk to indicate that the event is being held by hosts within the same organization. The resulting illustrations that accompany the designs are more colorful and intricate as to draw in attendees.

AstroDay

AstroDay is an event that celebrates astronomy with local communities through exhibitions and educational activities held by amateur and professional astronomers. The onset of COVID-19 changed this event from an in-person to a virtual platform to abide by social distancing regulations.
