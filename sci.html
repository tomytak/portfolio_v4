Astronomy through entertainment

The Canada-France-Hawai‘i Telescope requires a visual identity with supporting graphics for their collection of scientific videos for their annual Waimea Solar System Walk.

Waimea Solar System Walk

The annual Waimea Solar System Walk, in 2021, was altered to abide by social distancing rules due to COVID-19. The walk consists of a physical outdoor path in Waimea, Hawai‘i featuring educational videos—which attendees can view on Youtube—of Astronomers and Educators who inform viewers about each planet in our solar system.
